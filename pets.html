MY PETS!!!!

I like animals, but I love pets. Much to the chagrin of my friends, I can't stop talking about them. This is especially true of the family pets. Despite not being the one who picked any of them, I ended up taking care of all of them, a responsibility which I cherish.
I think they are cute, and I wanted to show them off to you.

Gerda

Gertrude, or Gerda as we call her in Russian, is our oldest pet at 10 years old. She is a teacup yorkie, and is roughly about the size of a sneaker on chicken legs. She was selected by my younger sister Eva at a flea market. Despite her size, she is fiesty and protective. If she doesn't like you, she will gnaw on your toes with her two remaining teeth. Or she may sic Ray on you as her bodyguard.

Ray

Rey, or as we nickname him at home 'Vah-Sya' (its a term of endearment in Russian), is our second oldest pet (7 years old), physically though not mentally. He is a German Shephard who my dad selected after our old GS dog 'Kai' died. Rey is a very sweet and playfull dog. Due to a bad experience with daycare in the past, he was left somewhat traumatized. To this day he is very cowardly and unsure of himself, despite being a very big dog. He prefers to lounge around at home, and doesn't like going outside or meeting new people or animals.

Stitch

Stitch, named after the Disney character, is a recent addition to the household at about 1 years old. He and his buddy Charlie where selected by my youngest sister Maya. A rambunctions clumsy oaf, Stitch is a very friendly and explorative cat. He likes hanging around people, or from the tops of furniture where he knocks things down from because he lacks his kinds normal grace. He is a rescue, so our only guess is he is an egyptian mau.

Charlie

Charlie, named because his coloration makes him look like the big Chaplin himself, was also a recent addition. Also a street cat, Charlie was found alone as a very young kitten. As such he is very much a scaredy cat. It takes allot to gain his trust, but when you do, he is extremly affectionate. He is always found with his adopted brother stitch, and will go nuts if separated from him for more then a few minutes, which I think is pretty rare for cats.
